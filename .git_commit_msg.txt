fix(frontend): replace Link with anchor in CookieBanner to fix white screen

CookieBanner is rendered outside <RouterProvider> in App.tsx.
Using react-router-dom's <Link> outside a Router context throws:
"TypeError: Cannot destructure property 'basename' of useContext(...)"
which crashes the entire React app, causing a white screen.

Fix: replace <Link to="/cookies"> with <a href="/cookies"> — a plain
HTML anchor is safe outside the router context and works correctly
for navigating to the cookies policy page.
